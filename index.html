Scheduling App

Group Project

Created a full-stack application using ReactJS with Redux designed to helped businesses keep track of employee clock-in/clock-out times, their full time-card, view requests for time off, and also where employees can go to request that time off. Information is pulled from and stored on a postgreSQL database. There are two different views, one for the employees and the other is for the manager. Database saves the information on whether or not the individual logging in is admin-level or not.
